> *"Write the vision and make it plain on tablets"* – **Habakkuk 2:2**

A digital journal inspired by the Jewish Messianic faith, where you can write, save, and organize **poems, thoughts, prayers, and Messianic writings** with an ancient parchment-style design, faith symbols, and a warm atmosphere.

🔗 **Live Demo:** [https://messianic-notebook.vercel.app](https://messianic-notebook.vercel.app) *(replace with your link after deployment)*

---

## ✨ Features

- ✍️ **Full editor** with 4 categories:
  - 🌸 Poetry
  - 💭 Thought
  - 🕯️ Prayer
  - 📜 Messianic Writing
- 💾 **Auto-save** in browser (localStorage)
- ✏️ **Edit** previously saved texts
- 🗑️ **Delete** writings with confirmation
- 🔍 **Filter by category** (All / Poetry / Thought / Prayer / Messianic)
- 📱 **Responsive design** – works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- 🕊️ **Jewish Messianic theme** with emojis, Bible verses, and symbolic icons (Menorah, Star of David, Dove)
- 🌙 **Local persistence** – your texts remain saved even after closing the browser
